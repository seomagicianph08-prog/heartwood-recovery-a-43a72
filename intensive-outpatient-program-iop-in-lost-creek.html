How long does the IOP last, and what is the weekly schedule like?
Our standard IOP typically runs for 8 to 12 weeks, with sessions held three to four days per week. We offer both daytime and evening groups, and our team will work with you to find a schedule that fits your needs. Length of stay can be adjusted based on your progress and clinical recommendations.
