Most clients are matched with a suitable sober living residence within 3 to 7 days after their initial consultation. Are the sober living homes you recommend licensed or certified in Texas?
Heartwood Recovery only refers clients to sober living homes that are certified by the Texas Recovery-Oriented Housing Network (TROHN) or meet national standards set by organizations like NARR (National Alliance for Recovery Residences). While Texas does not require all sober homes to be licensed, we prioritize placements in residences that uphold the highest quality and safety standards.

The process begins with a confidential assessment, either in person at our West Lake Hills office or virtually. Our team also coordinates with treatment providers to ensure a smooth transition and ongoing support during your stay.
